How to Generate Banners

  • Login to Lazada.hasoffers.com
  • Click "Offers" then "Browse/Search"
  • At the top of the page, go to "Browse by Category" and choose "Philippine offers"
  • Select the campaign banner you wish to promote
  • Scroll down, Copy & Paste the code to your website

This "Philippine Offers" list will show you all the running campaigns that you can promote. Just click on a campaign of your liking and you can get the banners for your blog/site.

How to Generate Links

  • Login to Lazada.hasoffers.com
  • Click "Offers" then "Browse/Search"
  • Search for "Deeplink" & Click "PH DEEPLINK GENERATOR"
  • On the Deeplink generator page, click "Add Deeplink"
  • Paste in your desired Lazada Landing Page URL
  • Click the "+" button and your tracking link will be generated

*Note your tracking links take around ~2 minutes to activate and start working after you generate them.
